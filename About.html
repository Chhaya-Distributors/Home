About Us

Your trusted partner in vaccine distribution for over 17 years

Our Story

Chhaya Distributors has been a cornerstone in the healthcare distribution industry for over 17 years, specializing in the supply of vaccines to medical facilities across the region. Our journey began with a simple mission: to ensure that quality vaccines reach those who need them most.

With our extensive experience and commitment to excellence, we have built strong relationships with healthcare providers and pharmaceutical companies alike. Our dedication to maintaining the cold chain and ensuring product integrity has made us a trusted name in vaccine distribution.

We understand the critical importance of timely delivery and proper handling of vaccines, which is why we've implemented rigorous quality control measures throughout our supply chain. Our team of experienced professionals works tirelessly to ensure that every vaccine reaches its destination in perfect condition.

Brahmanand Singh

Owner & Founder
